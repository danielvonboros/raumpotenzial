About Me

Architect & Interior Designer with a passion for creating exceptional spaces

I am an architect with over five years of experience in the construction and renovation of houses and apartments, as well as in the design of interior and exterior spaces. My focus is on combining functionality and aesthetic beauty—because spaces should not only look good, but also enrich the everyday lives of their inhabitants. My journey began with studying architecture, where I developed a deep understanding of spatial relationships, structural integrity, and the influence of environments on our well-being.

My journey began with studying architecture, where I developed a deep understanding of spatial relationships, structural integrity, and the influence of environments on our well-being. Throughout my professional career, I have been involved in numerous projects – from residential buildings to commercial spaces – always with a focus on the needs of the users. I have gained valuable experience, particularly in the maintenance, renovation, and contemporary redesign of apartments.

Over time, my focus shifted increasingly to designing individual pieces of furniture. This is where I discovered my passion for tailor-made solutions that are not only functional but also tell a story. This passion led me to specialize in interior concepts and interior design—because many rooms harbor untapped potential that can be revealed and experienced through intelligent planning. Changing circumstances often present us with new challenges. But especially in urban areas, where living space is scarce, a lot can be gained through well-thought-out concepts and targeted redesign. My approach is always personal and collaborative: every room should reflect the character and lifestyle of its inhabitants without losing its timeless elegance. Whether it's the transformation of an entire room or a single, striking statement piece of furniture, I combine traditional craftsmanship with modern design principles to create durable, high-quality results. Together, we develop a solution that perfectly suits your individual living situation.
